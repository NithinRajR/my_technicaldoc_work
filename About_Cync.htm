Cync is a SaaS based collateral management and loan monitoring application. It is dedicated to asset-based lenders and factors in the working capital lending space as well as other capital providers in commercial lending.

The application manages asset-based lending and factoring process where the portfolio manager maintains the entire process of uploading invoices, validating credit limits, maintaining credit scores, preparing verification letters and automatically calculating and recording advances, reserves and fees. It helps lenders to improve operational efficiencies, portfolio management, and borrower management. It makes processing and submitting Borrowing Base Certificates (BBCs) easier and faster for borrowers.
