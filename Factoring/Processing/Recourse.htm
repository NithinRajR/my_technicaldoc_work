Recourse

Invoices that exceed the configured recourse days in the Factoring Fee Setup automatically move to the Recourse grid after funding.

If an invoice has already exceeded its recourse days at the time of funding, the system routes it directly to the Recourse grid and skips the Funded Invoices grid.

Once an invoice appears in the Recourse grid, users can initiate recourse processing.

During recourse, the system applies the recourse fee defined in the Factoring Fee Setup along with any accrued fees.

Users can also apply additional charges, such as Wire Fees or Legal Fees, as part of the recourse process.

Fields & Descriptions:

Fields

Descriptions
Account Number

Displays the account number associated with the invoice.
Account Name

Displays the account name associated with the invoice.

The value appears as a hyperlink and allows users to navigate to

  • Invoice Details
  • Account Parameters
  • Comments
  • Invoice Documents.

Invoice Date

 Displays the invoice date for each invoice, formatted as “MM/DD/YYYY”.
Invoice Number

Displays the account number of the debtor associated with the invoice.
PO Number

Displays the purchase order number associated with the invoice.
Schedule Number

Displays the invoice schedule number. Clicking the hyperlink opens the Invoice DetailsInvoice Details HTML page.
Verification Number

Displays the verification number for the invoice.

Clicking the hyperlink opens the Verification Batch DetailsVerification Batch Details pop-up.
Invoice Amount

Displays the total amount of the invoice.
Invoice Date

Displays the invoice date, indicating when the product was delivered or the service was rendered.
Fund Date

Displays the date on which the invoice batch was funded, in MM/DD/YYYY format.
Due Date

Displays the invoice due date in MM/DD/YYYY format.

The due date is calculated based on the invoice date and the payment terms.

Example:
Invoice Date: 01/05/2021
Payment Terms: Net 30
Due Date: 01/31/2021
Fee Days

Displays the number of fee days applicable to the invoice.

Fee days are calculated based on the configuration in the Factoring Basic Parameters page and can be derived from either the Invoice Date or the Fund Date, depending on the setup.
Funding Batch

Displays the funding batch number associated with the invoice.
Invoice Amount

Displays the original invoice amount.
Balance Amount

Displays the outstanding balance of the invoice.
Advance Amount

Displays the advance amount funded for the invoice.

The advance amount is calculated using the advance rate configured in the Factoring Fee Setup page.

Example:
Invoice Amount: $10,000
Advance Rate: 80%
Advance Amount: $8,000.00

Note:

  • The Advance Rate accepts numeric values up to 200.
  • If a value greater than 200 is entered, the system displays the message: “Advance Rate must be less than or equal to 200.”
Reserve Amount

Displays the reserve amount retained for the invoice.
Accrued Fee

Displays the accrued fee balance for the invoice.

Accrued fee is calculated based on fee days and the applicable fee tier.

Formula:
Accrued Fee = Total Fee − Earned Upfront Fee
Recourse Amount

Displays the total recourse amount for the invoice, including accrued fees.

Formula:
Recourse Amount = Balance on Advance Amount + Recourse Fee + Accrued Fee
